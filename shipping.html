Shipping Policy

Australia-wide delivery through trusted fulfilment partners.

PawWash AU works with fulfilment partners to prepare and ship orders to Australian customers. Delivery estimates are shown clearly so customers know what to expect before they buy.

Processing and delivery times

  • Order processing usually takes 1-3 business days after payment is confirmed.
  • Estimated delivery to Australian addresses is usually 7-15 business days after dispatch.
  • Remote, regional, PO Box and high-volume delivery periods may take longer.
  • Tracking details are emailed when available. Tracking can take 2-5 business days to update after dispatch.

Fulfilment partners

Some orders may ship directly from our trusted fulfilment partners. If an order includes multiple items, parcels may arrive separately. This does not change your customer support or consumer rights with PawWash AU.

Shipping cost

  • Free shipping applies to eligible orders over A$63.
  • Shipping costs for smaller orders are shown at checkout before payment.
  • Prices are listed in AUD unless stated otherwise.

Address changes and delivery issues

Please contact us as soon as possible if your address is incorrect. Once an order has been dispatched, we may not be able to change the delivery address. If a parcel appears delayed, we will help check tracking and work with the fulfilment or delivery partner where possible.
