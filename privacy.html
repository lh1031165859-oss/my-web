Privacy Policy

How PawWash AU handles customer information.

This privacy policy explains what information we collect, why we collect it and how it may be used to process orders, provide support and improve the store.

Information we collect

  • Contact details such as name, email address, phone number and delivery address.
  • Order details including selected products, payment status, shipping status and support requests.
  • Technical information such as device, browser, pages visited and marketing attribution data.
  • Messages you send to us through email, forms or customer support channels.

How we use information

  • To process orders, arrange fulfilment, provide tracking and deliver customer support.
  • To send order updates, shipping notifications and service messages.
  • To improve the website, product pages, advertising and customer experience.
  • To detect misuse, prevent fraud and comply with legal obligations.

Service providers

We may share the information needed to complete an order with payment processors, fulfilment partners, delivery partners, customer support tools, analytics providers and advertising platforms. These providers are only given the information needed for their role.

Marketing and cookies

If we use Meta, TikTok, Google or similar advertising tools, they may use cookies or pixels to measure ad performance and improve campaigns. You can adjust cookie and advertising preferences through your browser and platform settings.

Marketing emails or SMS messages will only be sent where permitted. Every promotional message should include a way to unsubscribe.

Access and contact

You can contact us to ask about your personal information, request correction or ask for reasonable access where applicable. Email: support@pawwashau.com.
