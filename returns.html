Returns Policy

Clear returns for change-of-mind and product issues.

We want PawWash to feel fair and low-friction while keeping the policy realistic for a small online store using fulfilment partners.

30-day change-of-mind returns

You may request a change-of-mind return within 30 days of receiving your order. To be eligible, items must be unused, clean, undamaged and returned with the original packaging where possible.

  • Contact us before sending anything back so we can confirm the return instructions.
  • For change-of-mind returns, the customer is responsible for return postage.
  • Original shipping costs are not refunded for change-of-mind returns unless required by law.
  • Refunds are issued to the original payment method after the return is received and checked.

Faulty, damaged or not-as-described items

This policy does not limit your rights under Australian Consumer Law. If an item is faulty, damaged, not as described or fails to meet a consumer guarantee, you may be entitled to a repair, replacement or refund.

Please contact us with your order number, a short description of the issue and photos where helpful. We will review the issue and offer a fair solution based on the problem.

Product expectations

PawWash products are laundry helpers designed to catch loose pet hair, lint and fluff during the wash. No laundry tool can guarantee 100% pet hair removal. Results can vary depending on fabric, water level, washing machine type, load size and shedding amount.

Cancellations

If your order has not yet been processed or dispatched, we will try to cancel it and issue a refund. Once an order has been dispatched, it needs to be handled under the return process.
